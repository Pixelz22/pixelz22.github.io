Sound Design for "The Robbing"

SFX
Published Oct 24th, 2023

A short LEGO® stop-motion animation about a bank robbery made with my friend Connor (CQRB2).

I assisted with the script and vocal recordings, and handled all foley and audio production for the project. Connor wrote the majority of the script and handled all the animation. Some voice talents were provided by another friend, Isaac.

Remarks

  • Most SFX were created by handpicking clips from the internet and editing them to fit the scenario.
  • All vocals were recorded on my own personal Shure SM57 microphone.
  • This was my first time doing foley outside of a video game.

Tools & Frameworks

  • Ableton Live 12
  • Audacity
  • DaVinci Resolve

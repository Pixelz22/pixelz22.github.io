Developed Dec 18th, 2021 - Dec 19th, 2021

A top-down arcade-style game about cleaning up after messy customers in a hectic mall.

Move around the mall with WASD and try to pick up all the trash before the time runs out! You'll get different points for different types of trash, so try to get as much as you can!

This was made as a project for the ZenoJam of 2021 with my friend Jonathan over the course of 2 days. The game was made using the Unity 3D engine.

Remarks

  • This was my first published game that hadn't been made for a school project!
  • This entire game, including programming, art, and sound, was made in under 48 hours.
  • We were originally gonna change how the janitor cart worked, but we found a funny bug that let you slingshot around the map, so we left it as is.

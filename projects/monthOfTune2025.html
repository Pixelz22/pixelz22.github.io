Month of Tune 2025

Music
Published Nov 28, 2025

A compilation of short songs I wrote for the Month of Tune 2025 event.

Each day of June, we were given a theme, for which we were to write a short piece of music using any tools we wanted.

Remarks

  • My first digitally produced standalone song.
  • I had the inspiration for this song when sitting out on a lake, watching the sun break over the horizon.
  • The repetitive piano line and fading synths at the beginning creates a sense of building and anticipation that gets paid off by the strings and synths breaking into wide, gorgeous harmonies.
  • The song mixes atypical time signatures like 5/4 and 6/4 to create feelings of nature.

Tools & Frameworks

  • Ableton Live 12
  • MuseScore Studio
  • Audacity

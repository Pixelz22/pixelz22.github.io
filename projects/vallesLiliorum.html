Valles Liliorum in C Minor

Music
Written in 2018

A somber piano solo in 6/8 time.

This was one of my first major compositions, something that went beyond the basics. Unlike previous works, there was frequent use of harmony, and the true melody often swapped between the bottom and top of the harmony stack. Around this time I had taken a few music theory classes from Prof. Tom Lohr at Meredith College, and it shows in the cleaner notation, more sensible voicings, and more interesting chord progressions.

Remarks

  • This piece won the Intermediate category of the Cary-Apex Piano Teacher's Association Young Artist Award in 2018.
  • The name of the piece is Latin for "Valley of the Lilies."

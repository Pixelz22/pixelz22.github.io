Waltz of the Lilies

Music
Written in 2025

A different take on my other original work, "Valles Liliorum," now arranged as a waltz for string quartet.

I was revisiting my old pieces and wondered what it would look like if I redid them with my newfound compositional skills. "Valles Liliorum" seemed like a prime opportunity, as the left hand was doing simple triads practically the entire time. I changed to a waltz style and started adding simple countermelodies in the left hand, as I adore a rich bassline. I added more colorful extensions to the chords and a more intricate harmony. As I continued to work on the piece, it became clear my ideas went beyond the scope of solo piano. That's when I decided to make this my first string piece. This opened up the door for all my ideas and more.

Remarks

  • This piece was submitted to the NC State University Creative Artist Awards for 2025. No verdict has been issued.
  • My sister Kasey, a talented cellist, assisted me in making sure the song was physically playable, along with bowing suggestions.

Fiery Waters

Music
Published Jan 2nd, 2024

A song that mixes electronic and orchestral elements to create a beautiful soundscape imitating a sunrise.

Remarks

  • My first digitally produced standalone song.
  • I had the inspiration for this song when sitting out on a lake, watching the sun break over the horizon.
  • The song alternates uses atypical time signatures at the beginning to create feelings of nature.
  • The repetitive piano line at the beginning creates a sense of building and anticipation that gets paid off by the strings and synths breaking in with wide, gorgeous harmonies.

Tools & Frameworks

  • Ableton Live 12

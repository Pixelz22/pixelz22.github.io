A Halloween themed Catch 'Em-style game about getting candy from a ghost!

In this game, you control a small jack-o-lantern with your mouse and try to catch candy as it falls from the ghost above. Some candies give you points, others lose you points. The ghost also drops other things, like rocks or clocks, so you have to be careful to only pick up the things that will benefit you.

This was made as a project for my Game Design class at Wake Technical Community College. It was made with a group of 3 others. My responsibilities involved programming the ghost and player controls, UI programming, visual effects, the sound design and music, and implementing the sound in the game, along with assisting the other programmers with various game mechanics.

Remarks

  • There's a special intro to the game music that isn't included when it loops. This required extra programming to add.
  • This was the first instance of scene transitions I had ever implemented, making use of custom shaders and overlay images.
  • The Halloween theme was an idea based on the upcoming holiday.
